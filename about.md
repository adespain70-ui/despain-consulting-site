## The Approach

**Start with the problem, not the technology.** AI is powerful, but it's a tool. The real work is understanding your business deeply enough to know where that tool will make the biggest difference.

**Build for how you actually work.** Generic solutions gather dust. I create systems that fit your existing workflows, not the other way around.

**Transfer knowledge, not dependency.** My goal is to make your team more capable, not to create ongoing reliance on outside help.
